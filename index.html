Hello, I'm Heather

A UI/UX designer passionate about creating intuitive digital experiences that solve real problems for users and businesses.

About Me

With over 7 years of experience in UI/UX design, I specialize in creating user-centered digital products that are both beautiful and functional. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative problem-solving to deliver experiences that delight users and drive business results.

I've had the privilege of working with startups and enterprise companies across various industries including Beef 'O' Brady's, KJ's Steakhouse, and e-commerce. This diverse experience has given me a unique perspective on how design can address different user needs and business challenges.

My design philosophy centers around empathy, simplicity, and attention to detail. I believe that great design should be invisible, allowing users to accomplish their goals effortlessly while creating memorable moments along the way.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new festivals, experimenting with photography, or attending design meetups to stay connected with the creative community.

My Design Process

My approach to design is methodical yet flexible, allowing me to adapt to each project's unique requirements while ensuring consistent, high-quality outcomes.

1

Research & Discovery

Understanding the problem space through user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to establish a solid foundation.

2

Define & Ideate

Synthesizing research insights to define user needs and business goals, then generating multiple solution concepts through collaborative ideation.

3

Design & Prototype

Creating wireframes, visual designs, and interactive prototypes that bring the solution to life while maintaining focus on user needs.

4

Test & Iterate

Validating designs through user testing and feedback, then refining the solution based on insights to ensure it meets user needs effectively.

My Design Philosophy

User-Centered

I believe in designing with empathy, always putting the user's needs and goals at the center of every decision.

Purposeful

Every element in my designs serves a purpose. I focus on creating experiences that are both functional and delightful.

Accessible

I'm committed to creating inclusive designs that can be used and enjoyed by people of all abilities and backgrounds.
